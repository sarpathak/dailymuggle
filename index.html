Andrej Karpathy Trains GPT-2 in Pure C

April 10, 2024

Andrej Karpathy, a prominent figure in the field of artificial intelligence, has made a noteworthy contribution with his recent project, llm.c. This project breaks away from the conventional methods of training large language models (LLMs) and offers a unique approach: training a GPT-2 model entirely in C, bypassing the popular deep learning framework, PyTorch. It's efficient and portable due to C's nature but it can be complex for larger models and lacks the functionality of established libraries.

Meta launches Mind reading Wristband

March 15, 2024

At F8 2024 conference, Meta unveiled a mind-blowing prototype: a wristband translating brain signals into AR/VR controls. This BCI tech could revolutionize human-computer interaction. Imagine exploring VR worlds just by thinking! While in early stages, it holds immense potential for diverse fields, from entertainment to healthcare. Challenges like data security and ethics exist, but Meta's innovation marks a giant leap towards a future where our thoughts control the digital world.
